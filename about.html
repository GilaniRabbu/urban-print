About us

Urban Print, a beacon of contemporary fashion, has illuminated the retail landscape for half a decade. Founded on a vision of providing high-quality, trend-setting clothing, the store has blossomed into a trusted name in the fashion industry. With branches in vibrant cities like Tbilisi, Kazan, Bursa, Grozny, and Adana, Urban Print is a true testament to the global appeal of its offerings. At Urban Print, we pride ourselves on curating a diverse collection that caters to various tastes and lifestyles. Our carefully selected range of clothing is a fusion of urban chic and timeless elegance, ensuring that every customer finds something that resonates with their individual style.

What truly sets Urban Print apart is its unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. The positive reviews pouring in from our loyal clientele serve as a testament to the exceptional shopping experience we offer. Our dedicated team goes above and beyond to provide personalized assistance, ensuring that each customer leaves with a smile. Moreover, Urban Print places a strong emphasis on quality and sustainability. We source materials and work with manufacturers who share our commitment to ethical practices, creating a positive impact on both our customers and the environment.

As we celebrate five years of success, Urban Print remains dedicated to pushing boundaries and setting new trends in the fashion industry. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our customers, whose support and trust have been instrumental in our journey. With your continued patronage, we look forward to many more years of redefining style and leaving a lasting mark on the world of fashion.
